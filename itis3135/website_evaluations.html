Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple's website is a great example of modern, clean, and user-friendly design.

  • Contrast: Strong contrast between text and background allows readability.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of typography, spacing, and layout across pages.
  • Alignment: Everything is aligned perfectly with clear sections and white space.
  • Proximity: Related elements are grouped together logically for easy navigation.

Yale School of Art

Yale School of Art is part of the prestigious Yale University, and has a website that feels cluttered and outdated.

  • Contrast: Bad color choices and overlapping text make readability an issue.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent fonts, spacing, and alignment make the site feel disorganized.
  • Alignment: Elements are scattered across the page with no clear structure.
  • Proximity: Important information is spread out randomly, forcing users to search for what they need.
